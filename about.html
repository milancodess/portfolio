About Me

Hello! I'm Milan Bhandari, a passionate web developer currently in Grade 12. My journey into web development started a few years ago when I discovered my love for creating functional and aesthetically pleasing websites. I have since worked on various projects, ranging from personal blogs to small business websites.

I am constantly learning and improving my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks and libraries. I strive to create websites that not only look good but also provide a great user experience.

In addition to web development, I have a keen interest in graphic design and photography, which helps me bring a creative touch to my projects. I am excited to continue my journey in this field and look forward to working on more challenging and rewarding projects.
